Improving Electrolaryngeal Speech Enhancement via a Representation Learning Method Based on Integrated Text and Speech Representations

Authors: Ding Ma, Jinyi Mi, Fengji Li, Lester Phillip Violeta, Kazuhiro Kobayashi, Tomoki Toda
Comments: Submitted to IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (IEEE EMBC 2025).
Abstract:

Main concept

EL2SP dataset

Source EL data, which is an EL corpus recorded from a male laryngectomee; Target normal data, which is a nomral courpus recorded from a healthy male speaker.

Six types of speech were evaluated.

  • Referenced speech:
    • Source: Source EL speech (EL).
    • Target: Target normal speech (SP).
  • Converted speech from Baseline 1, Baseline 2.
  • Converted speech from Proposed 1, Proposed 2.
